About

I’m a Data Scientist & ML Engineer who ships end-to-end: data pipelines, models, and production services. I care about clean interfaces, measurable outcomes, and reliability.

Recently: Data Science Analyst Intern @ Icon Life Sciences (May–Sept 2025) — built a BigQuery golden source (12+ systems), delivered brand-level forecasts (MAPE 7.9%), and reduced stock-outs. Before that, I worked on CNN/OCR microservices at scale (p95 < 300 ms @ ~1k req/s) and completed a LiDAR re-localisation final year project exploring ICP robustness.

I’m based in Dublin. Outside of tech, I co-founded FarmEasy, a backyard-to-table micro-farm, and I’ve served as treasurer for student societies, managing ≈ €25,000.
