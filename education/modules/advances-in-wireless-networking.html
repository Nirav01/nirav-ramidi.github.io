Learning Outcomes

  • Analyse link budgets, SNR, and capacity for different bands (sub-6, mmWave).
  • Explain OFDMA/MU-MIMO scheduling and channel access (contention vs scheduled).
  • Compare Wi-Fi 6/7 features with 5G NR numerologies and network slicing.
  • Design low-latency pipelines with MEC/edge offloading and pragmatic QoS.
  • Evaluate IoT stacks (BLE, Zigbee, LoRaWAN) and application protocols (MQTT/CoAP).

802.11ax/ax+ (Wi-Fi 6/7)

Key ideas:
- OFDMA (RU allocation), MU-MIMO
- BSS colouring to reduce CCA deferrals
- Target Wake Time (power saving)
- 6 GHz band (Wi-Fi 6E) & wide channels

Wi-Fi 7 adds 320 MHz, multi-link operation (MLO), higher modulations.
