Information Visualisation

Design and build effective visualisations using the Vega-Lite grammar. Focus on perceptual principles, interaction (brushing & linking), and tidy data transforms to support exploratory analysis and clear communication.

Learning Outcomes

  • Select appropriate visual encodings (position, length, color) for data types and tasks.
  • Compose dashboards with brushing & linking, selections, and small multiples.
  • Use Vega-Lite transforms (aggregate, bin, calculate, fold, window) to tidy data.
  • Apply perception & accessibility guidelines to reduce bias and improve readability.
  • Communicate findings with concise captions, legends, and progressive disclosure.

Indicative Topics

Encodings & Marks

Bar, line, point, area; channels (x/y, color, size, shape) and effectiveness ranking.

Interaction

Selections, interval brush, cross-filtering, highlighting, tooltips.

Composition

Layering, concatenation, faceting, small multiples; resolve scales and legends.

Tidy transforms

aggregate, calculate, fold, bin, window for ranks and rolling stats.

Perception & Accessibility

Color/contrast checks, alignment, annotation, avoiding misleading scales.
