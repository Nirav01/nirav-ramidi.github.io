Golden-Source & Forecasting

Unified 12+ data silos into a BigQuery golden source and shipped brand-level LightGBM forecasts with MAPE 7.9%, helping reduce CNS stock-outs by 18%.

  • BigQuery + Airflow
  • MAPE 7.9%
  • 108 SKUs · 25 therapy areas
  • Excel effort −80%

Context & Goals

Sales, inventory, and Rx data lived across disparate systems (ERP, CRM, e-commerce, distributor reports). Analysts spent hours reconciling extracts and definitions varied by team. The goal was to establish a single source of truth in BigQuery and deliver reliable brand-level forecasts for planning.

  • Unify data from 12+ systems covering 108 SKUs across 25 therapy areas.
  • Replace manual Excel workflows (target: −80% analyst time).
  • Produce quarterly demand forecasts with MAPE ≤ 10%.

Architecture

Ingestion

  • Cloud Functions to normalise CSV/API feeds and land to staging tables.
  • Idempotent loads with file hashing & partitioned tables.

Orchestration

  • Airflow DAGs for daily/weekly jobs with retry & SLA alerts.

Transform

  • SQL transforms to conformed dims (product, region, channel) and fact tables (orders, Rx, inventory).
  • Audit columns, data quality checks, referential integrity reports.

Serve

  • Model-ready marts and Looker/BI dashboards for commercial & supply teams.

Forecasting Approach

Built a brand-level forecasting pipeline using LightGBM with calendar, promotion, and lag features. Baselines included naive seasonal and moving-average models.

  • Features: lags/rolling stats, holiday/calendar flags, stock-in/out indicators.
  • Validation: time-series CV (expanding window); error reported as MAPE.
  • Result: MAPE 7.9% (median across brands) vs baselines > 12%.

Outcomes & Impact

  • Excel effort −80% via scheduled pipelines and conformed marts.
  • Stock-outs −18% in CNS brands as planning adopted forecast signals.
  • Stakeholder RoB: weekly ops & monthly exec reviews with dashboard snapshots.
  • Shared a data dictionary and lineage docs to align definitions across teams.

Lessons & Next Steps

  • Semantic consistency beats model tweaks: a clean product hierarchy reduced unexplained variance.
  • Add dbt for versioned transformations; introduce Great Expectations checks.
  • Trial probabilistic forecasts (QR or NGBoost) for service-level planning.
